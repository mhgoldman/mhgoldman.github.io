Hi, I'm Martin.

I'm a technology consultant and manager specializing in client-facing technical service delivery, web development, and cloud computing.

About Me

My family got our first computer from Radio Shack in the late '80s. It was a Tandy 1000 with a 286 processor, and I was 5. I've been hooked ever since.

I graduated from Binghamton University in 2005, with a double major in computer science and music. After a brief stint at Goldman Sachs and a failed startup, I spent 7 years building and managing a client-facing solutions engineering team at Capital IQ, which is now part of S&P Global. Since 2015, I've been part of the Professional Services team at Skytap, a cloud infrastructure startup.

I love to get my hands dirty using interesting technology to solve customers' problems, with a special interest in web development and cloud-based infrastructure. I also have extensive experience in building global, customer-facing engineering teams and functions from the ground up.

Other things I enjoy include stand-up comedy, choral music and podcasts.
